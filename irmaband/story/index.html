The story of I.R.M.A.

I.R.M.A. aka Intercontinental Rocktastic Missile Act is a dream come true for the founding three lifelong metal heads.
The band was formed on four principles:

① Wears masks because it's not about egoism, it's about the best music

② We are the Brian Eno of Heavy Rock, meaning the band will never play live shows

③ Releases singles only, because the world doesn't need more filler songs

④ After the launch of our first few singles, many of our songs will have a surprise feature

Let the music tell the rest of the story!
Forever yours,
