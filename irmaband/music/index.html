Our Music

Our second single 'Boo the Boundless Love' is out now on all your favorite streaming services! It’s a shuffle-beat heavy rock banger telling a sarcastic true story about a dude whose life was too full of love and lost his ability to write lyrics as a result. The band is pleased to report the dude has since recovered from this nasty illness.

Also check our previous single! Knife Play is an anthemic heavy rock banger telling a true story about a dude who fell in love with a wrong person, and it almost cost his life. Love was stabbed dead, but life went on.
